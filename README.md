# Hi there, I'm Rodrigo! 👋

I'm a **Research and Development Engineer** specializing in **Scientific Visualization**.
With a **PhD in Electrical Engineering from Unicamp**, my work focuses on interactive 3D graphics rendering, digital image processing, and visualization software development.

### 🔬 Research & Development

My expertise lies in developing tools that translate complex scientific data into intuitive visual experiences.
I have extensive experience with:

- **Medical Imaging:** Processing and rendering volumetric data for diagnostic and treatment planning purposes.
- **Engineering Simulations:** Visualizing mechanical waves and petroleum reservoir simulation data to support advanced studies in solid mechanics and petroleum engineering.
